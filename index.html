WHAT’S NEW IN GGV IDEATHON 2023

Calling innovators! GGV Ideathon is here, inviting students, researchers, startups, and incubators to showcase their ideas across 5 tracks: Innovation, Invention, Process, Business Idea, and Product Prototype. Win ₹21,000 for the best entry and ₹11,000 for runner-up in each category.
